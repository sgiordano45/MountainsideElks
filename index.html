More Than a Lodge — A Family

Since 1958, Mountainside Elks Lodge #1585 has been serving our community through charitable programs, scholarships, veterans initiatives, and more. We've provided millions of dollars in support to local causes.

But we're not all work — we're also a place where lifelong friendships are formed. From karaoke nights to beefsteak dinners, there's always something happening at the lodge.
