About Our Lodge

Over 65 years of service, fellowship, and community impact in Mountainside, NJ.

Our Mission

The Benevolent and Protective Order of Elks was founded in 1868 on the principles of Charity, Justice, Brotherly Love, and Fidelity.

At Mountainside Lodge #1585, we bring these principles to life every day through charitable giving, community service, and genuine fellowship. We believe that by working together, we can make our corner of the world a better place.

"To inculcate the principles of Charity, Justice, Brotherly Love and Fidelity; to recognize a belief in God; to promote the welfare and enhance the happiness of its Members; to quicken the spirit of American patriotism; to cultivate good fellowship... Elks Care and Elks Share." — BPOE Mission Statement

Our History

Mountainside Lodge #1585 was chartered in 1958, making us one of the established fraternal organizations in Union County. For over six decades, we've been a cornerstone of the Mountainside community.

Over the years, our lodge has provided millions of dollars in charitable support to local causes, from student scholarships and veterans programs to supporting Camp Moore — the NJ State Elks Special Project designed specifically for children with physical and developmental challenges.

Today, our 200+ member lodge continues to grow, welcoming new members who share our commitment to service and community.

Our Charitable Programs

Making a difference in our community and beyond

Student Scholarships

Each year we award scholarships to deserving local students pursuing higher education. Our scholarship program has helped dozens of young people achieve their academic goals.

Veterans Services

We're committed to supporting those who served. Our veterans programs include hospital visits, care packages, and financial assistance for veterans in need.

Camp Moore

As sponsors of Camp Moore, the NJ State Elks Special Project, we send children with physical and developmental challenges to camp at no cost to their families.

Hoop Shoot

Our annual Elks Hoop Shoot gives local youth the chance to compete in a free-throw shooting contest, with winners advancing to state and national levels.
