Membership Requirements

The Elks welcome all American citizens who meet our basic requirements.

  • Be a U.S. Citizen

    Must be a citizen of the United States of America.

  • Be at Least 21 Years Old

    Applicants must be 21 years of age or older.

  • Believe in God

    The Elks is a non-denominational organization that requires belief in God.

  • Be of Good Character

    Applicants must be of good moral character.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to know a current member to join?

While you do need a sponsor, you don't have to know one beforehand. Contact us and we'll connect you with a member who can sponsor your application.

Can women join the Elks?

Absolutely! The Elks has welcomed both men and women as full members since 1995. All members have equal standing and voting rights.

How much time commitment is required?

That's entirely up to you! Some members volunteer extensively, while others simply attend social events. We appreciate whatever time you can give.

Can I bring my family to events?

Yes! Many of our events are family-friendly. We also have events specifically for members and their guests. The Elks is a family-oriented organization.

What if I move to another area?

You can easily transfer your membership to any of the 1,800+ Elks lodges nationwide. Your membership travels with you!
